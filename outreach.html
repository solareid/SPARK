Outreach & education

Connecting solar physics, space weather and society

SPARK will turn mission science into public engagement, educational resources, student opportunities and citizen-science participation.

Programme

Mission science for schools, museums and the public

The SPARK outreach programme will coordinate public talks, remote events, exhibitions, classroom activities and teacher training across participating countries.

Outreach ambassadors will help disseminate content nationally, with exhibition material available in the languages of participating countries.
