Team & collaboration

An international consortium with deep mission heritage

SPARK brings together solar physics, high-energy instrumentation, EUV imaging, software, operations and modelling expertise across Europe and international partners.

Consortium structure

Distributed leadership across two instrument consortia

HiFI consortium

HiFI is led by UCL Mullard Space Science Laboratory, with distributed Co-PI leadership and contributions spanning optical design, calibration, sensors, electronics, mechanisms, software and data pipelines.

LASSAN consortium

LASSAN is led by the Leibniz Institute for Astrophysics Potsdam, with major European contributions to detectors, anticoincidence shielding, Co-STIX, software, calibration and electronics.

Heritage

Built on proven space-mission experience

The consortium draws on extensive heritage from Solar Orbiter/EUI and STIX, RHESSI, SOHO, PROBA-2, Hinode, JUICE, Euclid and other major space projects.
